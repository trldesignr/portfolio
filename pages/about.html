About Me

Outside of design, I enjoy all climates and environments of the outdoors. Watching movies, and listening to music while I do everything. Cooking and eating. And riding my motorcycle. I am also very fond of all things Star Wars (feel free to engage me about the stories/movies... but be prepared to go deep).

My Design Process

My design process is objective. Focusing on defining the problem to a fault. To a point that the problem becomes so clear, that the solution becomes obvious. Establish a clear directive and approach, then drive it end-to-end.

No matter the product or company, I come to the table with a perspective that product design and design systems are as much about the business and organization as they are about the end-user.
